Honey-Garlic Chicken Thighs

Description

Crispy and saucy all at the same time. Honey-Garlic Chicken Thighs is a simple recipe made with crisped chicken thighs and sauce. The sauce is a perfect blend of honey and garlic. This dish is easy to make and simple enough to whip up quickly.

Ingredients

  • 8 (5 ounce) boneless chicken thighs with skin
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 Tablespoons olive oil, or as needed
  • 1/2 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 7 cloves garlic, chopped or to taste
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 pinch onion powder, or to taste
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Season the chicken on both sides with salt and pepper
  2. Cover the bottom of a cast iron skillet with olive oil and bring to medium heat.
  3. Add chicken and brown on one side, 3 to 5 minutes
  4. Flip chicken, and add onion and garlic
  5. Continue to cook until chicken is mostly (but not fully) cooked and onion and garlic are soft, 6 to 7 minutes more
  6. Remove chicken to plaet
  7. Add honey, soy sauce, onion powder, and garlic powder to skillet
  8. Stir and scrape the bottom of the pan with a wooden spoon to get the garlic and onion to mix with the liquid
  9. Put chicken back into the pan, cover and reduce to medium heat. Cook until no longer pink in the center and juices run clear, about 10 minutes, turning once halfway through. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center of a thigh should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C)
  10. Place on a serving tray and drizzle liquid from the pan on top. Sprinkle with chopped cilantro before serving
